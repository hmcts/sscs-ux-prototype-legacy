Sending evidence to support your appeal.

Evidence includes anything which shows how your condition affects your life. For example, a doctor’s letter or report. A written statement by yourself or someone who knows you can also help the tribunal understand your appeal.

Please send in any evidence as soon as possible after you’ve submitted your appeal. This is so there’s time to read it before your hearing.

We’ll send you an address where you can post your evidence. You’ll receive this by [email / post] after you’ve submitted your appeal.
