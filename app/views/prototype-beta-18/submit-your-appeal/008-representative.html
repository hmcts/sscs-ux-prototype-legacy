Do you have a representative?

Your representative can be anyone who’s helping you with your appeal and you think should be kept updated, such as a welfare rights adviser, family member or carer.

Registering them means they can:

  • receive personal information about your appeal
  • submit evidence (and see any that’s submitted)
  • act on your behalf during your appeal

Do you have someone who you’d like to be the representative on your appeal?

If you don’t have the details to hand, you can phone up and register a representative after you’ve submitted your appeal.
