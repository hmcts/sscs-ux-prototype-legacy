Mandatory Reconsideration

Six weeks later they receive a Mandatory Reconsideration Notice (MRN) from the DWP. It says they have looked at the decision again but they are sticking by the original decision. He is still not entitled to the PIP benefit.

It’s quite a long letter and Manish doesn’t understand all of it. But he sees that he is able to appeal to an independent tribunal online. Frustrated about not being listened to and anxious about the appeal process, he and his wife go online and search for ‘Appeal benefit decision’.
