Sending evidence to support your appeal

Evidence includes anything which shows how your condition affects your life, such as a letter from your doctor or healthcare professional.

You or someone who knows you could also write a statement. It all helps the independent tribunal understand your appeal.

Send in any evidence as soon as possible after you’ve submitted your appeal. This is so there’s time to read it before your hearing.

Only send evidence which you have not already sent to DWP.

You’ll get a letter and an email [if email given] telling you the address to post your evidence a few days after submitting your appeal.
