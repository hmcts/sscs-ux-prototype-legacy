Do you have a representative?

You’ve said you are an ‘appointee’. You may also have someone who’s helping with your appeal and you think should be kept updated, such as a welfare rights adviser.

Registering them means they can:

  • receive information about your appeal
  • submit evidence (and see any that’s submitted)
  • act on your behalf during your appeal

Do you have someone who you’d like to be the representative on your appeal?

If you don’t have their contact details to hand, you can contact the tribunal and register them after you’ve submitted your appeal.
