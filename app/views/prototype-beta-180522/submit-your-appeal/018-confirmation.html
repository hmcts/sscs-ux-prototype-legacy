What happens next

DWP will be notified that you want to appeal against their decision. They have until 26 June 2018 to respond. You’ll be told the next steps when DWP have responded.

The tribunal may need to contact you and ask you some additional questions. It’s important that you reply as soon as you can.

You’ll receive an email and an SMS to confirm you have submitted your appeal. You’ll get another email and SMS within 3 working days with an address to post your evidence and a link so you can track your appeal.
