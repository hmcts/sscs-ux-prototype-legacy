Social Security and Child Support (SSCS)

Manish appeals a benefit decision

Manish receives a Mandatory Reconsideration Notice (MRN) from DWP. It tells him they are sticking by their original decision to award his benefits at a reduced rate. He feels he is entitled to more.

The MRN letter tells him he can appeal the decision through an independent tribunal. There's a web site address for the Appeal a benefit decision service on GOV.UK.

He visits the web site and to find out how he can appeal the DWP decision.
