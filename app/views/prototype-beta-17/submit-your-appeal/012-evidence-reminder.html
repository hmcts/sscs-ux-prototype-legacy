Sending evidence to support your appeal

Evidence includes anything which shows how your condition affects your life. For example, a doctor’s letter or report. A written statement by yourself or someone who knows you can also help the tribunal understand your appeal.

Post any evidence to the address below. Make sure you include your name and National Insurance number:

Birmingham ASC
HM Courts and Tribunals Service
Administrative Support Centre (ASC)
P O Box 14620
Birmingham
B16 6FR

We’ll send you this address again after you’ve submitted your appeal.
