Sending evidence to support your appeal

Evidence is anything which shows how your condition affects your life at the time of the assessment by DWP. For example, a doctor’s letter or report. A written statement by yourself or someone who knows you and your condition can also be useful.

You can post evidence to us separately after you’ve submitted the appeal. You should send it as soon as possible so there’s time to read it before your hearing.

We’ll send you details of where to send evidence after you’ve submitted your appeal.
