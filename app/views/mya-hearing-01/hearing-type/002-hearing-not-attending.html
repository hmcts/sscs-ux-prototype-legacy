You have chosen not to attend the hearing

You can still send in evidence to support your appeal. You’ll be sent an address where you can post it. You’ll receive this by [email / post] after you’ve submitted your appeal.

If you change your mind about attending the hearing, you can write to or phone the tribunal. The contact details will be on the first email or letter you receive after submitting your appeal.

Please note that you will not receive any email or SMS updates about your appeal because you are not attending a hearing.
