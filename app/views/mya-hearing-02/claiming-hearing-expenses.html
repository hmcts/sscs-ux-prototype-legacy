Claiming expenses to attend your hearing

You may be able to claim back some of the money you spend on attending your hearing.

You should use public transport where possible. Keep your travel tickets and bring them with you to your hearing. You’ll be given a claim form to fill out by the tribunal clerk.

If you have a health condition or disability which means you cannot get yourself to the hearing then you might be able to claim for a taxi. You’ll need to get authorisation first by phoning 0300 123 1142.
