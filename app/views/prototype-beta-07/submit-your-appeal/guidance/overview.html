1. Overview

You can use this service to appeal a decision that was made about your entitlement to benefits. For example Personal Independence Payment (PIP), Employment and Support Allowance (ESA) or Housing Benefit (HB).

This is an independent service that will decide whether the decision was correct.

Before you appeal

For most benefits you have to ask the DWP to reconsider their decision before you appeal. This is known as requesting mandatory reconsideration.
