Help and support

Help with appealing

There are organisations that can help with your appeal such as the Citizens Advice Bureau (CAB), Disability Rights or Welfare Rights.

They can advise you on the law and help you manage your appeal if you feel you can’t do it yourself.

Appealing by post

The vast majority of appeals are made by filling out and posting in a paper form. You’ll be taken to the right form when you start your appeal.

If you’re appealing an Employment and Support Allowance decision you can make your appeal online or by downloading a paper form. It’s quicker and more convenient to apply online.

Financial support

The appeal can take several months. You can apply for financial support if you think you’ll struggle for money during this time.
