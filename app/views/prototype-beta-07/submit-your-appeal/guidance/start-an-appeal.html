Start an appeal

Use this service to appeal against a decision that was made about your entitlement to benefits. For example Personal Independence Payment (PIP), Employment and Support Allowance (ESA) or Housing Benefit (HB).

The tribunal is independent and will decide whether the decision about your benefit was correct.

Before you start

For most benefits you have to ask the DWP to reconsider their decision before you appeal. This is known as requesting mandatory reconsideration.

If you’re appealing a decision about Housing Benefit then you should phone your local authority before appealing. For Child Benefit you should phone HMRC first.

To use this service you’ll need:

  • your Mandatory Reconsideration Notice
  • your National Insurance number
  • details of anyone who’s supporting you with your appeal. Such as a representative or friend or family member.
