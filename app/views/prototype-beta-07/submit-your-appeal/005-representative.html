Do you have someone who you’d like to be the representative on your appeal?

Such as a welfare rights adviser, carer or friend.

This means they’ll be able to:

  • receive the same information as you do about your appeal
  • submit evidence (and see any that’s submitted)
  • help present your appeal at your hearing
If you don’t have the details to hand, you can phone up and register a representative after you’ve submitted your appeal.
