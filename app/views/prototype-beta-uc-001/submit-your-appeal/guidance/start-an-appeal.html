Start an appeal

Use this service to appeal a benefit decision that you don’t agree with. For example, Personal Independence Payment (PIP), Employment and Support Allowance (ESA) or Universal Credit.

To appeal you’ll need:

  • your Mandatory Reconsideration Notice (read it before you appeal)
  • any evidence you want to upload (you can also post it in later)
  • your National Insurance number (this will be on any letter about the benefit)
  • details of anyone who’s supporting you with your appeal, such as a representative or friend or family member
