Do you want to register a representative?

Your representative can be anyone who’s helping you with your appeal and you think should be kept updated, such as a welfare rights adviser, carer or family member.

Registering them means they can:

  • receive information about your appeal
  • submit evidence (and see any that’s submitted)
  • act on your behalf during your appeal

Do you have someone who you’d like to be the representative on your appeal?

If you don’t have their contact details to hand, you can register them after you have submitted your appeal.
