Sending evidence to support your appeal

If you have any evidence, it should be sent to us as early as possible to avoid delaying your appeal.

Evidence is anything which shows how your condition affects you at the time of the assessment by the DWP. Such as a letter, assessment or report from a doctor.

You can send this to us separately after you’ve submitted your appeal.

We’ll send you details on how to submit evidence after you’ve submitted your appeal.
