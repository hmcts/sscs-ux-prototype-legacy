Evidence to support your appeal

Evidence is any information that supports your appeal such as a letter, written statement or medical report. Useful evidence shows how your condition affects your life.

You can upload evidence now or after you have submitted your appeal. You can also post it in. You’ll be sent the address after you’ve submitted your appeal.
