Sending evidence to support your appeal

If you have any evidence, you should send it as early as possible to avoid delaying your appeal.

Evidence is anything which shows how your condition affects your life at the time of the assessment by DWP. For example, a doctor’s letter or a written statement by yourself or someone who knows you.

You can send this to us after you’ve submitted the appeal. We’ll send you details of how to do this by post [if email provided] and email.
