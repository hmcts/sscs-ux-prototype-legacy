Do you have a representative?

Such as an adviser or carer who’s helping you with your appeal.

Registering them means they’ll be able to:

  • receive personal information about your appeal
  • submit evidence (and see any that’s submitted)
  • talk to us on your behalf about your appeal
If you don’t have the details to hand, you can phone up and register a representative after you’ve submitted your appeal.
