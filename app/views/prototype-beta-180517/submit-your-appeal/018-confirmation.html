What happens next

DWP will be notified that you want to appeal against their decision. They have until 6 June 2018 to respond. You’ll be told the next steps when DWP have responded.

The tribunal may need to contact you and ask you some additional questions. It’s important that you reply as soon as you can.

If you’ve signed up to email or text messages you’ll get one within 3 working days with a link so you can track your appeal.
