Sending evidence to support your appeal

Evidence includes anything which shows how the claimant’s condition affects their life, such as a letter from their doctor or healthcare professional.

You or someone who knows them could also write a statement. It all helps the independent tribunal understand the appeal.

Please send in any evidence as soon as possible after you’ve submitted your appeal. This is so there’s time to read it before the hearing.

You’ll get an email and a letter telling you the address to post it after you’ve submitted your appeal.
