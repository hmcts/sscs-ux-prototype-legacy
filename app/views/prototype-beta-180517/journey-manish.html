Social Security and Child Support (SSCS)

Manish

Scenario

Manish receives a Mandatory Reconsideration Notice (MRN) from DWP. It tells him they are sticking by their original decision to award his benefits at a reduced rate. He feels he is entitled to more.

The letter tells him he can appeal the decision through an independent tribunal. There's a web site address for the Appeal a benefit decision service on Gov.uk.

He visits the web site and sees that he can appeal the DWP decision online. He completes the appeal form and receives an email confirming he's appeal has been received.
