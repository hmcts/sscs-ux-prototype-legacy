Your appeal has been submitted

What happens next

DWP will be notified that you want to appeal against their decision. They have until 17 December to respond. You’ll be told the next steps when DWP have responded.

The tribunal may need to contact you and ask you some additional questions. It’s important that you reply as soon as you can.

[If signed up to email only] You’ll get an email within 3 working days to confirm your appeal has been lodged. This will have a link so you can track your appeal online. You’ll also get a copy of your appeal in a separate email.

[If signed up to email and SMS] You’ll get an email and text message within 3 working days. They’ll have a link so you can track your appeal online. You’ll also get a copy of your appeal in a separate email.

[If signed up to SMS only] You’ll get a text message within 3 working days. This will have a link so you can track your appeal online. You’ll receive a copy of your appeal in the post when DWP respond to your appeal.

[If not signed up to email or sms] You’ll receive a letter confirming your appeal in the post within 7 working days. You’ll also get a copy of your appeal from DWP when they respond.
