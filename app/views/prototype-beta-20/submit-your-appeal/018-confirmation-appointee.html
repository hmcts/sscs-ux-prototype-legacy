What happens next

We’ll notify DWP of your appeal. They have until 17 November 2017 to respond. We’ll contact you and explain the next steps when they reply.

We may need to contact you and ask you some additional questions. If we do, it’s important that you reply as soon as you can.

[If signed up to email] You’ll get an email from us within 3 working days. This will have a copy of your appeal and a link so you can track your appeal online.

[If signed up to email and SMS] You’ll get an email and text message from us within 3 working days. They’ll have a link so you can track your appeal online. The email will have a copy of your appeal.

[If signed up to SMS] You’ll get a text message from us within 2 working days. This will have a link so you can track your appeal online. You’ll receive a copy of your appeal in the post when DWP respond to your appeal.

[If not signed up to email or sms] You’ll receive a letter confirming your appeal in the post. You’ll also get a copy of your appeal from DWP when they respond.
