4. Start an appeal

Use this service to find the right form for your benefit appeal.

You can appeal online if you’re appealing a Personal Independence Payment (PIP) decision.

To appeal you’ll need:

  • your Mandatory Reconsideration Notice
  • your National Insurance number
  • details of anyone who’s supporting you with your appeal. Such as a representative or friend or family member
