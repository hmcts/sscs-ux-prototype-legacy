Sending evidence to support the appeal

If you have any evidence, it should be sent to us as early as possible to avoid delaying the appeal.

Evidence is anything which shows how the person’s condition affects their life. Such as a doctor’s report or letter. Or a written statement by someone who knows them.

You can send this to us after you’ve submitted the appeal. We’ll send you details of how to do this by post and email, if you’ve provided an email address.
